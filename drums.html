shakers&rattles

They may call spirits in sacred ceremonies or emphasize the words and actions of important people. Some examples of these instruments are djembe shakers, the kosikas, the caxixi, juju shakers, ankle shakers etc.

bells

Just like the rattles, bells often have ritual or ceremonial functions in traditional societies. Their rhythm often lets you immediately recognize the music's ethnic origin.

xylophone

There's a surprising variety of African xylophone cultures as it is one of the continents most central instruments. An example is the balafon, which recites the whole history of the Mande people.
